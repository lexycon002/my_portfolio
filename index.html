About me

Hi there! I am Awowole Hammad Olamilekan, a dedicated frontend web developer who thrive on the art of crafting immersive online experiences. My journey in the vast landscape of web development has equipped me with a diverse skill set that i'm eager to leverage for every project.


In the realm of markup and structure, I wield the power of HTML to create the backbone of web content. Cascading Style Sheets (CSS) and SCSS are my artistic palettes, allowing me to breathe life into designs with visually captivating and responsive layouts.


JavaScript is my language of choice when it comes to interactivity and dynamic functionalities. Also javaScript framework such as React is one of the tools i used to create dynamism in a website. Through its versatility, I can enhance user experiences and ensure seamless navigation on digital platforms. Another important area of my versatility in integration of Application programming Interface(API) which help me to create a beautiful website.


In the realm of collaboration and version control, Git and GitHub are my trusted companions. They allow me to seamlessly manage code, collaborate with others, and keep projects organized and efficient.


When it comes to showcasing my digital masterpiece to the world, I'm well-versed in the deployment process. Platforms like Github Pages, Netlify and Vercel serve as my launchpads.
